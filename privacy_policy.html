Privacy Policy

App: My Workout  |  Last updated: 2026-05-23 (added CSV import section)

My Workout is a local-only workout logging app. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data we collect

We collect no personal data. The app does not collect, transmit, or share any information about you or your device.

Data stored on your device

All workout data you enter (routines, exercises, sets, weights, notes) is stored locally on your device using an on-device database. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it yourself.

Data export

The app provides an optional export feature that lets you save your workout data as a CSV or JSON file. Exports are triggered entirely by you and are handled by your device's built-in share sheet. We have no access to exported files.

Data import

The app provides an optional CSV import feature. When you use it, the app opens your device's built-in file picker and reads only the file you explicitly select. Imported data is stored locally on your device in the same on-device database described above. No file content is transmitted to any server or third party.

Internet access

The app sends no data to any server. If you paste a URL into a notes field and tap it, the app opens that link in your device's default browser. The app itself makes no network requests and does not control or collect data from any external site you visit.

Third-party services

The app uses no third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services.

Children's privacy

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
